Donating to Condres OS

Condres OS is funded by our community. This is how we put the donations to use:

  • Web hosting and bandwidth costs.
  • Supporting Open Source projects that Condres OS depends upon.
  • Eventually pay for full time developers of Condres OS .

Patreon is a unique way to fund an Open Source project. A regular monthly income ensures sustainability for the Condres OS project. Patrons will be rewarded with exclusive project news, updates and invited to participate in video conferences where you can talk to the developers directly.

If you would prefer to use PayPal then we have options to donate monthly or make a one off donation.
